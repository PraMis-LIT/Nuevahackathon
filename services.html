  • Stress Outcomes in Daily Life

    Anxiety is a disorder that can affect everything you do in your daily life. It is not something you can quickly recover from, like a cold or stomach bug. Many people with anxiety think they are just feeling sad, and that it will go away with time. For some people, it does just that. But for 15 million others, anxiety is a constant feeling that does not go away on its own. These people are the ones who may benefit from extra support and help. In fact, the World Health Organization (WHO) found that anxiety is the second greatest reason for disability in the world. Unfortunately, only 10% of these people ever receive any effective treatment.
