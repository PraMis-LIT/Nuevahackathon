ABOUT SOCIALMED

Core Values of our website

Respect

We expect everyone to respect each other as people and have friendly conversations.

Safe

We want everyone who uses our website to feel safe in their secure chat rooms and not have the fear of being bullied.

Positivity

We expect all our users to show positivity in their respective chats.

Support

We want everyone to feel supported with whatever they talk about.

Chat Rooms

We created chat rooms so people can talk to others when
they need help. They can access therapists whenever they
need through this website as well as talk to other people
who are also looking for friends or are willing to help.
