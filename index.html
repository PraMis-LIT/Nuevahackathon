ABOUT SOCIALMED

Core Values of our website

Respect

We expect everyone to respect each other as people and have friendly conversations.

Safe

We want everyone who uses our website to feel safe in their secure chat rooms and not have the fear of being bullied.

User-Friendly

Our services are made by kids like you, so we understand that havinga complicated webiste should not be there but instead there should be a simple environment. If you want to make a suggestion to the website you can say it in the Contact Us section.

Support

We have a support team that will be there for you anytime. This team would be a bot that can respond to you and then if you want to be connected to a real person you can request that too!
